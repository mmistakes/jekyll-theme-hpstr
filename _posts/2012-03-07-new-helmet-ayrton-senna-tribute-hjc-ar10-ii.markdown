Hey everyone,

Motorsport season is about to get started here in the northwest and a few months back I was in the market for a new helmet. After doing my research I finally decided on a HJC AR10-II. This helmet overall was comfortable, and still looked pretty good for the price. Another bonus was the fact it had a different variety of cheek pads to choose from.

<a href="http://domofactor.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/hjc_ar10ii_white.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-6966" title="hjc_ar10ii_white" src="http://domofactor.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/hjc_ar10ii_white.jpg" alt="" width="500" height="241" /></a>

Having recently seen the new movie/Documentary Senna(click <a href="http://domofactor.com/2011/09/ayrton-senna/">here</a> for my review), I decide I would paint my helmet with the same overall theme as Senna's as a tribute.
<a href="http://domofactor.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/529669_3641422841658_143056403_n.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8910" title="529669_3641422841658_143056403_n" src="http://domofactor.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/529669_3641422841658_143056403_n.jpg" alt="" width="612" height="612" /></a>

<!--more-->

I contacted Nick Kennell over at <a href="http://sinistairkustoms.wordpress.com/" target="_blank">Sinistair Kustoms</a> who I knew through my friend to see if he would be able to come up with a design similar to that of ayrton's without copying it completely. The end result was amazing, and so far I'm really happy with it. Here's some pics that show the journey the helmet went through, along with the end result. Enjoy!
