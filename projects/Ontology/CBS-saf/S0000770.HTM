Natural Progression (Class)
Description: "Projected Property" attributing a condition or state which is expected to occur in the absence of any intervention.
Projected Outcome (Class)
Description: "Projected Property" attributing a condition or state as a potential effect of an "Activity".
Comment:
This is something which, at the time of deduction, does not exist but is predicted so to do, as distinct from an actual outcome which is deduced to exist at the time of observation.


This is not the same as the result (finding) of an observing activity.
