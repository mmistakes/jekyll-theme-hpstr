System Architect Symbol details for "Observed Property"
Actual Property (Class)
Description: "Property" applicable at or before time of attribution.
AP/Deduction (Generalization Node)
Base Class: "Actual Property"
Definitions Used:
Actual Property (Class)
Hypothesis (Class)
Description: "Actual Property" established by a process of reasoning based on one or more other properties of this "Subject" or other directly or indirectly related "Subjects".
Comment:
Properties may be deduced only from the existence of other properties. These may be associated either with the target SUBJECT or with one or more other, associated, SUBJECTS.
Example.
A GP identifies that a patient has an "elevated temperature" because they have a
temperature of 101 degrees.

Observed Property (Class)
Description: "Actual Property" established by direct sensation or measurement.
Comment: Establishment may be by human senses or machine.
