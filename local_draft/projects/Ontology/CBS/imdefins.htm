Image Valued Characteristic Defining Diagram
"Subject Characteristic" having a value represented by an image.
The image may be video or audio and may be static or moving. Examples would include:
Scans
X rays
Videos of colposcopies etc
Recordings of sounds or conversations.
Attributes:
Image (M) (Image)
Video or audio image.
