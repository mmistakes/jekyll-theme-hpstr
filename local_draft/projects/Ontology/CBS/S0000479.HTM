Derived Agent (Class)
Description: "Authorised Agent" derived from another (the origin).
Comment: A professional person ("Authorised Agent" through professional licence) becomes an employee of a hospital, a "Derived Agent".
Derived Agent By Agreement (Class)
Description: A "Derived Agent" who is empowered by some agreement or act.
Comment: This covers most employment and contractual arrangements.
Derived Agent By Decree (Class)
Description: A "Derived Agent" created by some authority without the necessary agreement of the source "Agent".
Comment: This may imply some degree of coercion.
